About Us

At Café Lumière, we specialize in creating soulful moments. From carefully sourced coffee beans to artisanal pastries, everything we serve reflects our dedication to quality, community, and comfort. Whether you're working, relaxing, or meeting friends, our café is your space to shine.

Our Coffee Journey

Every cup tells a story. We travel the world to source exceptional beans from sustainable farms, building relationships that span generations.

Ethiopia

Yirgacheffe Region

The birthplace of coffee. Our Ethiopian beans deliver bright, floral notes with hints of bergamot and jasmine. Hand-picked at high altitude, these heirloom varieties are processed using traditional washed methods.

Floral Citrus Tea-like

Colombia

Huila Department

Grown in volcanic soil at 1,800 meters, our Colombian beans offer perfect balance. Rich caramel sweetness meets crisp acidity, with notes of red apple and brown sugar. A crowd favorite for espresso.

Caramel Nutty Smooth

Our Roasting Philosophy

We roast in small batches every week to ensure peak freshness. Our roast master carefully develops each origin's unique characteristics, bringing out the flavors that make each coffee special. From light to dark, every roast is crafted with precision and passion.
